What is included in the SEO Audit?

The SEO Audit includes a detailed review of your website's current SEO performance. We analyze aspects such as page speed, keyword usage, site structure, backlinks, and more. You’ll receive a comprehensive report with actionable insights to improve your website’s visibility on search engines.

How will the SEO Audit help my website?

Our audit identifies areas of improvement that can help your site rank higher on search engines like Google. By addressing issues like slow page load times, poor keyword optimization, or broken links, you can improve user experience and attract more organic traffic.

What is the training like?

The training is a 2-hour, one-on-one session where we guide you through the process of implementing the recommendations from your SEO audit. You’ll learn how to make changes to your website, optimize content, and monitor SEO performance. Our goal is to empower you to manage your website’s SEO effectively.

While the SEO audit and training focus on empowering you with the knowledge to make changes, we do offer additional consulting services for implementation. You can contact us for hourly consulting if you need further assistance with applying the audit recommendations

SEO is a long-term strategy, and changes you implement from the audit can start showing results within a few weeks to a few months. However, consistent effort and optimization are key to long-term success. The audit will give you a solid foundation for ongoing improvement.
